# Roadmap

Mise_Deck should stay focused, fast, readable, offline-first, and useful during real kitchen work.

## v1.6.0

- Unified English and Portuguese/Brazilian firmware
- Selectable color themes
- Smoother rendering and long-name scrolling
- Shared PT-BR/English recipe compatibility
- Stability and control cleanup

## Next release

- Scale module integration
- Guided bowl placement and tare workflow
- Ingredient-by-ingredient weighing
- Visual progress bar and audio feedback
- Completion flow returning to the recipe overview

## Future ideas

- Recipe search
- Custom category management
- Recipe templates
- Improved Cardputer-side backup controls
- Thermometer module integration
- Further battery and charging refinements where supported by the hardware

## Principles

- Offline-first
- microSD-friendly
- Readable on a small screen
- Fast during real kitchen use
- Open and hackable
- Cyberdeck-flavored without sacrificing usability
